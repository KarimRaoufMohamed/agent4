# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Purpose
The Business Requirements Document (BRD) serves as a high-level document outlining the business needs, objectives, and goals for the "Business Expert AI Agent Builder" project. It is essential for aligning stakeholders on the project's scope, expectations, and requirements, ensuring that all parties have a mutual understanding of what the project aims to achieve.

## Audience
This document is primarily intended for business stakeholders, project managers, and technical teams involved in the development and implementation of the "Business Expert AI Agent Builder." 

## Key Components

### Executive Summary
This section provides a brief overview of the "Business Expert AI Agent Builder" project, detailing its purpose of enabling users to build and manage AI agents through an intuitive onboarding wizard and central dashboard. The project aims to streamline business processes by leveraging AI technology.

### Business Objectives
- To develop an intuitive web application that allows users to create and manage AI employees seamlessly.
- To enhance operational efficiency by automating business tasks through AI capabilities.
- To provide users with robust support in building their AI teams by offering AI-driven suggestions and tools integration.

### Scope of Work
**In Scope:**
- Development of onboarding wizard with a 4-step flow.
- Creation of the AI Team Dashboard for managing agents.
- Implementation of various tabs for agent overview, skills, tools, knowledge, memory, and activity logs.
- Integration of Telegram and connection management for various tools.
- User authentication functionality.

**Out of Scope:**
- Development of AI agents beyond the preset roles listed.
- Integration with non-specified tools or platforms outside the initial scope.

### Requirements
- **Functional Requirements:**
  - Enable users to describe their business and receive AI-generated descriptions.
  - Allow users to select multiple capabilities for AI assistance.
  - Provide a grid to select agent roles and create agents.
  - Display detailed agent overview, including performance metrics and skills management.
  - Implement tool management and integration features.

- **Non-Functional Requirements:**
  - The application must be user-friendly and accessible on various devices.
  - Ensure robust security measures during user authentication and data handling.

### Stakeholders
- **Project Sponsor:** Responsible for overall project vision and funding.
- **Project Manager:** Overseeing the project timeline, budget, and scope.
- **Development Team:** Responsible for building and implementing the application.
- **QA Team:** Ensuring the application meets all functional and non-functional requirements.
- **User Experience Designer:** Focus on the usability and design aspects of the application.

### Assumptions and Constraints
- It is assumed that the necessary APIs for tool integration will be accessible and functional.
- The project timeline is constrained by the availability of resources and third-party tool integrations.
